## NANOTECH

**Nanotechnology --** the miniaturization of technologies that accommodate a new class of interfaces with both the human body and the physical world.

### Future Headline
*"Diabetes Type 1 successfully edited out of an embryonic genome"*

### Nanofabricator / Molecular Fabricator

**Nanofactory** -- (**Molecular Manufacturing) --** uses massive arrays of nanometer-scale actuators (produced initially through self-replication) to manufacture macroscale products with atomic precision.

### Autonomous Nanoscale Objects

Autonomous nanoscale objects are:
- Self-healing Polymers
- Self-replicate
- Self-assemble
