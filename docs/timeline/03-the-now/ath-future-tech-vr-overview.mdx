## REALITIES (XR / VR / AR) / IMMERSIVE TECH

**Realities** - an immersive multimedia that creates an environment which simulates physical presence in places in the real or imagined world and allows the user to interact with objects and entities in that world. VR creates sensory experiences that can mimic sight, sound, touch, and smell.

### Key Concepts

**Presence / Immersion**
- **Inducing flow states of consciousness**
- **Dream states**
- **Fear training**

**The Digital Physical Soup** -- the blurring of the digital and physical realms by projecting digital items onto the physical landscape, while also having the ability to convincingly simulate the physical "real" environment with sensory stimulus.

People learn through interaction; we can make abstract things concrete in VR. 2030 will be a digital, physical soup where kids grow up with a broader range of senses and understanding, poking holes in reality.

**Digital Twin -** An evolving digital profile of a process or asset's past and current behavior enables predictive capabilities and more informed planning.

A science fiction scenario that takes place within a multiverse, where iterations of selves evolve.

**Empathy Machine --** experiences are more than documentaries; they offer opportunities to walk a mile in someone else's shoes from their perspective.
